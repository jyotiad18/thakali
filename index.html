Welcome To Sherchan Thakali Foods

The look, aromatic smell and the taste, is the generation passed inherited secret of the Thakali community when it comes to their culture food along with their rich ethnic culture values. Thakali food is not only about the taste and spices, it is the culinary art heritage passed over to the generations, the techniques, timings and quality of incidents to use to cook.

Sherchan Thakali Foods brings you the centuries old inherited culinary menu, art of cooking and taste on your plate with the best available local ingredients and spices to droll your food experience in cultural taste.
